J1 Summer Work & Travel

Step 1: Registration

Requirements

  1. Must be 18—29 years old.

  2. Must pay a non–refundable registration fee of JMD $2500.

  3. Must be a full-time student at a tertiary institution.

  4. Must have successfully completed at least one semester with at 2.0 GPA.

  5. 2 passport size photos (not certified).

  6. 1 copy of passport information page.

  7. 1 copy of resume.

  8. Copy of school ID.

  9. Certified (signed & stamped) student status letter from institution and transcript.

  10. Completed application form and self- introductory video.

A non-refundable deposit of USD $100 must be paid two weeks after registration to secure your application.

Step 2: Application Process

  1. Medical exam and police record ( a full health report and a criminal record must be done no earlier than than December and submitted by the end of February).

  2. Submit transcript & deposit program fee of US $600 by December 31st.

  3. Total Program Fee Cost: New/Returning Students:

    • $1100/$1060usd for full placement

    • $1200/$1160usd for Job fair

    • $885/$850usd for self placement

NCB A/C #304855711 Half Way Tree branch. All lodgment slips must be kept and presented to our office for payment confirmation. Payment can be made in installments. Jobs are not guaranteed until full payment of program fee. Program fee is to be paid in US dollars ONLY and DOES NOT include embassy application or appointment fees. Deadline for entire program fee is February 28.

Program fee covers:

  • Pre-screening & application processing.
  • Job placement and verification.
  • DS-2019 Form (Work Permit).
  • Medical/health insurance coverage(only lasts for program duration).
  • Embassy interview pre preparation.
  • In country advice assistance.
  • Sevis fee.

ADDITIONAL FEES THAT WILL APPLY:

  • Orientation pre-departure & post arrival.
  • Visa appointment and application.
Cancellation Policy:

75% of program fee will be reimbursed in the event of cancellation from the program. Cancellation details are provided in the student contract.
