Mission Statement

We aim to provide a premise for young Jamaicans to advance through participation in various cultural exchange programs overseas, to increase their adaptability to different work cultures and to increase their marketability across the global sphere. International Exchange Agency (IEA) is a registered partnership entity under the Employment Agencies Regulation Act of Jamaica. IEA has been in operation since September 2013, and since then has been offering international placements to qualified college/university undergraduate and graduate applicants. We aspire to obtain growth and development in our services offered, to serve our applicants best and build solid relationships with our business partners and associates.
